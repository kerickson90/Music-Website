About this Site

Each month I post what I'm listening to here. This site was built with plain html, css, and Bootstrap 4, as well as embedded links to Spotify. I don't own any of the music but I do have a monthly premium Spotify subscription which allows me to copy the song links via Spotify desktop's application. I listen to a wide variety of genres, but tend to trend towards top-40s/pop music :) Hope you enjoy! -Katherine
